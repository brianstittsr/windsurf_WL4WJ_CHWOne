# Regional Information

Information about NC CHWA regions and regional resources.

## Overview

North Carolina is divided into 8 CHWA (Community Health Worker Association) regions, each serving specific counties with tailored resources and support.

## NC CHWA Regions

### Region 1 - Western NC

**Counties Served:**
- Cherokee, Clay, Graham, Haywood, Jackson, Macon, Swain
- Buncombe, Henderson, Madison, Polk, Rutherford, Transylvania

**Regional Focus:**
- Rural health access
- Mountain community outreach
- Appalachian health initiatives

### Region 2 - Northwest Piedmont

**Counties Served:**
- Alleghany, Ashe, Avery, Mitchell, Watauga, Wilkes, Yancey
- Burke, Caldwell, Catawba, McDowell

**Regional Focus:**
- Rural-urban health bridges
- Agricultural worker health
- Small town health services

### Region 3 - Piedmont Triad

**Counties Served:**
- Alamance, Caswell, Davidson, Davie, Forsyth, Guilford
- Randolph, Rockingham, Stokes, Surry, Yadkin

**Regional Focus:**
- Urban health disparities
- Immigrant health services
- Chronic disease management

### Region 4 - Triangle

**Counties Served:**
- Chatham, Durham, Franklin, Granville, Orange, Person
- Vance, Wake, Warren

**Regional Focus:**
- Research partnerships
- Health technology
- Diverse population health

### Region 5 - Sandhills

**Counties Served:**
- Anson, Hoke, Lee, Montgomery, Moore, Richmond
- Scotland, Stanly, Union

**Regional Focus:**
- Military family health
- Rural health access
- Agricultural health

### Region 6 - Cape Fear

**Counties Served:**
- Bladen, Brunswick, Columbus, Cumberland, New Hanover
- Pender, Robeson, Sampson

**Regional Focus:**
- Coastal health issues
- Disaster preparedness
- Indigenous health

### Region 7 - Eastern NC

**Counties Served:**
- Carteret, Craven, Duplin, Greene, Jones, Lenoir
- Onslow, Pamlico, Wayne, Wilson

**Regional Focus:**
- Agricultural worker health
- Coastal community health
- Rural health infrastructure

### Region 8 - Northeast NC

**Counties Served:**
- Beaufort, Bertie, Camden, Chowan, Currituck, Dare
- Edgecombe, Gates, Halifax, Hertford, Hyde, Martin
- Nash, Northampton, Pasquotank, Perquimans, Pitt
- Tyrrell, Washington

**Regional Focus:**
- Rural health access
- Health equity
- Community partnerships

## Regional Resources

### Accessing Regional Pages

1. Navigate to **Collaborations > Regional Pages**
2. Select your region
3. View regional resources and information

### Regional Content

Each regional page includes:
- Local CHW directory
- Regional events
- Training opportunities
- Partner organizations
- Health statistics
- Resource listings

## Regional Dashboards

### Region 5 Dashboard

The Region 5 dashboard provides:
- Interactive county map
- CHW distribution data
- Health metrics
- Resource locations

### Dashboard Features
- **Map View** - Visual county representation
- **Statistics** - Regional health data
- **Directory** - Local CHW listings
- **Events** - Upcoming activities
