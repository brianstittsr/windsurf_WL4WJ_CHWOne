# Certification Information

Track and manage your CHW certification status within CHWOne.

## Understanding CHW Certification

Community Health Worker certification validates your training and competency. CHWOne helps you track certification status and receive renewal reminders.

## Certification Levels

Based on WL4WJ standards, there are multiple certification levels:

### Entry Level
- **Requirements:** Basic training completion
- **Hours:** 40-80 training hours
- **Focus:** Core competencies

### Standard Level
- **Requirements:** Entry level + field experience
- **Hours:** 80-120 training hours
- **Focus:** Specialized skills

### Advanced Level
- **Requirements:** Standard level + leadership training
- **Hours:** 120+ training hours
- **Focus:** Supervision and training others

### Master Level
- **Requirements:** Advanced level + extensive experience
- **Hours:** 160+ training hours
- **Focus:** Program development and policy

## Adding Certification Details

1. Go to **My Profile**
2. Navigate to the **Certification** tab
3. Enter your certification information:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Certification Number | Your official certification ID |
| Certification Status | Current status |
| Issue Date | When certification was granted |
| Expiration Date | When certification expires |
| Certifying Body | Organization that issued certification |

## Certification Status Types

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Active** | Valid and current |
| **Pending** | Application in progress |
| **Expired** | Past expiration date |
| **Suspended** | Temporarily inactive |
| **Revoked** | Permanently cancelled |

## Renewal Process

### Step 1: Check Requirements
Review your certifying body's renewal requirements:
- Continuing education hours
- Supervised practice hours
- Documentation needed

### Step 2: Complete Requirements
- Attend approved training sessions
- Log continuing education credits
- Gather required documentation

### Step 3: Submit Renewal
Contact your certifying body to submit renewal application.

### Step 4: Update CHWOne
Once renewed:
1. Go to **My Profile** > **Certification**
2. Click **Edit**
3. Update expiration date
4. Update certification number if changed
5. Click **Save Changes**

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I add my certification?
Go to Profile > Certification tab > Edit > Enter details > Save

### What if my certification expired?
Update your status to "Expired" and begin the renewal process with your certifying body.

### Can I have multiple certifications?
Currently, CHWOne tracks your primary certification. Contact support for multiple certification tracking.

### Who can see my certification?
Visibility depends on your privacy settings. By default, certification status is visible to organizations you work with.
