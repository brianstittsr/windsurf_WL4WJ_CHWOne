# Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with CHWOne in minutes.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Navigate to the CHWOne homepage
2. Click **Register** in the top navigation
3. Choose your registration type:
   - **Community Health Worker** - For individual CHWs
   - **Organization** - For nonprofits and agencies
4. Complete the registration form with your information
5. Verify your email address

## Step 2: Complete Your Profile

After logging in for the first time:

1. Go to **My Profile** from the sidebar
2. Fill in your basic information:
   - Name and contact details
   - Profile photo
   - Professional bio
3. Add your certification details (for CHWs)
4. Set your service area and expertise
5. Configure privacy settings

## Step 3: Explore the Dashboard

The dashboard provides an overview of your activity:

- **Quick Stats** - Key metrics at a glance
- **Recent Activity** - Latest updates and notifications
- **Quick Actions** - Common tasks you can perform

## Step 4: Start Using Tools

Depending on your role, you'll have access to different tools:

### For CHWs
- **Profile** - Manage your professional information
- **Referrals** - Track client referrals
- **Forms** - Complete health assessments
- **Resources** - Find community services

### For Organizations
- **Dashboard** - Organization overview
- **Projects** - Manage health initiatives
- **Grants** - Track funding opportunities
- **Reports** - Generate data insights
- **AI Assistant** - Get intelligent help

## Need Help?

- Click the **Platform Tour** button on any page for a guided walkthrough
- Use the **AI Assistant** to ask questions about features
- Contact your organization administrator for support
