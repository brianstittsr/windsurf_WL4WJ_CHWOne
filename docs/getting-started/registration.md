# User Registration

This guide walks you through the registration process for CHWOne.

## Registration Types

### Community Health Worker (CHW) Registration

CHWs can register individually to:
- Create a professional profile
- Track certification status
- Access referral management
- Connect with organizations

**Required Information:**
- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Password (minimum 8 characters)
- NC County of residence
- Certification number (if certified)

### Organization Registration

Organizations (nonprofits, agencies) can register to:
- Manage multiple staff members
- Access full platform tools
- Track grants and projects
- Generate reports

**Required Information:**
- Organization name
- Primary contact name
- Business email
- Phone number
- Organization type
- Service area

## Step-by-Step Registration

### 1. Access Registration Page

Navigate to `https://yoursite.com/register` or click **Register** from the homepage.

### 2. Select Registration Type

Choose between:
- **I am a Community Health Worker**
- **I represent an Organization**

### 3. Complete the Registration Form

#### For CHWs:

```
Personal Information
├── First Name *
├── Last Name *
├── Email Address *
├── Phone Number *
└── Password *

Professional Information
├── Certification Number
├── Certification Status
├── Expiration Date
└── Primary County

Service Area
├── Region Selection
├── Counties Served
└── Languages Spoken
```

#### For Organizations:

```
Organization Details
├── Organization Name *
├── Organization Type *
├── Tax ID / EIN
└── Website

Contact Information
├── Primary Contact Name *
├── Email Address *
├── Phone Number *
└── Address

Service Information
├── Service Area
├── Programs Offered
└── Staff Size
```

### 4. Verify Your Email

After submitting the form:
1. Check your email inbox
2. Click the verification link
3. You'll be redirected to login

### 5. First Login

Upon first login:
1. Complete any remaining profile fields
2. Take the optional platform tour
3. Set up your preferences

## Password Requirements

Your password must include:
- At least 8 characters
- One uppercase letter
- One lowercase letter
- One number
- One special character (recommended)

## Troubleshooting

### Email Not Received?
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Verify the email address entered
- Request a new verification email

### Registration Error?
- Ensure all required fields are completed
- Check that email isn't already registered
- Try a different browser

### Forgot Password?
- Click "Forgot Password" on login page
- Enter your registered email
- Follow the reset link

## Data Privacy

Your registration data is:
- Encrypted in transit and at rest
- Never shared without consent
- Subject to our Privacy Policy
- Deletable upon request
