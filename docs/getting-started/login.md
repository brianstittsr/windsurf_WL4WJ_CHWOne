# Logging In

Access your CHWOne account securely.

## Standard Login

### Step 1: Navigate to Login

Go to the login page:
- Click **Login** from the homepage
- Or navigate directly to `/login`

### Step 2: Enter Credentials

1. Enter your **email address**
2. Enter your **password**
3. Click **Sign In**

### Step 3: Access Dashboard

After successful login:
- You'll be redirected to your dashboard
- Or to the page you were trying to access

## Remember Me

Keep yourself logged in:
1. Check **Remember me** before signing in
2. Your session will persist for 30 days
3. You won't need to log in each visit

> **Note:** Don't use on shared computers

## Forgot Password

Reset your password if forgotten:

### Step 1: Click Forgot Password
On the login page, click **Forgot password?**

### Step 2: Enter Email
Enter the email address associated with your account

### Step 3: Check Email
1. Check your inbox for reset email
2. Check spam/junk if not found
3. Click the reset link

### Step 4: Create New Password
1. Enter your new password
2. Confirm the password
3. Click **Reset Password**

### Step 5: Login
Use your new password to log in

## Two-Factor Authentication

If 2FA is enabled on your account:

### Step 1: Enter Credentials
Log in with email and password as normal

### Step 2: Enter Code
1. Open your authenticator app
2. Find the CHWOne entry
3. Enter the 6-digit code
4. Click **Verify**

### Setting Up 2FA
1. Go to **Settings > Security**
2. Click **Enable 2FA**
3. Scan QR code with authenticator app
4. Enter verification code
5. Save backup codes securely

## Login Troubleshooting

### Invalid Credentials

If you see "Invalid email or password":
- Verify email is correct
- Check caps lock is off
- Try resetting password

### Account Locked

After multiple failed attempts:
- Wait 15 minutes
- Or reset your password
- Contact admin if persistent

### Email Not Verified

If prompted to verify email:
1. Check inbox for verification email
2. Click verification link
3. Try logging in again
4. Request new verification if needed

### Session Expired

If your session expires:
- You'll be redirected to login
- Log in again to continue
- Consider using "Remember me"

## Security Best Practices

1. **Strong passwords** - Use unique, complex passwords
2. **Enable 2FA** - Add extra security layer
3. **Log out** - Sign out on shared devices
4. **Check URL** - Verify you're on the real site
5. **Update regularly** - Change password periodically

## Multiple Accounts

If you have multiple accounts:
- Use different browsers
- Or use incognito/private mode
- Log out before switching

## Mobile Login

Logging in on mobile devices:
1. Open browser on your device
2. Navigate to CHWOne
3. Tap **Login**
4. Enter credentials
5. Tap **Sign In**

## SSO Login (If Enabled)

For organizations with Single Sign-On:
1. Click **Sign in with SSO**
2. Select your organization
3. Enter organization credentials
4. You'll be redirected back
